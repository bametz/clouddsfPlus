Hierarchical Layout

The hierarchical layot shows the structure of CloudDSF+ with Decision Points, Decisions and Outcomes. For easier navigation all elements can be toggled at once with the respective controls.

Decision Relations Layout

The visualization show all decision of CloudDSF+ clustered by their respective decision point. All identified relationship types can be toggled through the respective controls. Clicking on a decision will highlight its outgoing decisions.
